LULU Stock, October 13 2023.

Posted by Christopher Bai on December 21 2023

Introduction:

Hello, I'm Christopher Bai an 11th grader at the Lawrenceville School in NJ. I have been investing for 4 years now and I am investing at an early age to for long term goals.


The trade: Shares of LULU at 377.69 on October 13, 2023.

I was debating purchasing LULU stock at ~$379. LULU stock was not hitting a low by all means and was coming off a high growth period and was up 25% from March 2023-October 2023. However, I believed in the growth of the stock due to the company’s innovation and interest in fast growth, the stock would still grow. I decided upon assessing the company mission and recent initiatives. I found out that Lululemon was going to lead its partnership with the Canadian National Team going into the 2028 Olympics in LA. I saw this as a showing for the company’s growth and I believed that partnership would show shareholders the high upside potential of the company. Through the company’s third quarter fiscal report, I also saw that Lululemon’s men’s apparel market was currently following a 5-year growth plan to double revenue by 2026 by quadrupling international sales while doubling digital and menswear sales. I believed this would be the perfect time to jump in on the stock as a long term holding. However, I did see that the P/E ratio of the stock was higher than I usually like at 28.49. P/E ratio is the ratio of the price of the stock and the earnings per stock meaning that a low P/E signifies that the stock is undervalued whereas a higher P/E signifies overvaluation. But like I said earlier, I was pretty confident that the rising industry in athletic wear and recent initiatives would relieve my concerns. I was hesitant and wasn’t betting on an immediate return but I knew this stock would hold long term and I purchased LULU stock at ~379$.
