Welcome to Financial Literacy

Hello everyone, I’m Christopher Bai and I’m here to welcome you all to Piggy Banking’s course on financial literacy. Financial literacy is not just the simple idea of saving up money. There’s many more layers to it which we’ll discuss in the later units. Money used without any planning can quickly turn bad, but if you know how to use it it can be a very powerful tool. Being financially literate can sound daunting at times but I assure you that as long as you are planning everything out, it will work out. In addition to the course materials, you'll find hands-on exercises. These activities let you put what you've learned into practice in your own financial life. Even the smallest steps you take to improve your financial know-how are a big deal. When you use what you've learned, you're building a strong foundation for a better financial future. Again I’m excited you all are here with us and this is the first step to becoming Financially literate, let’s get right into it.
